About Us

JSL Creations has been in operation since 2007 providing online marketing services to businesses looking to increase their online reach. After years of operating as a small scale performance and content marketing agency, it was acquired by a much larger agency with it's founder, Jared, accepting a senior network manager position where he managed a portfolio of the network's largest accounts.

Given their strong understanding of performance marketing metrics and analytics, they were able to streamline workflow and operational processes at the network yielding substantial increases in network bottomline and exploding revenues.

After a successful tenure there, Jared decided to return to his roots of operating his own agency, retaining the talent of his original team. Currently JSL Creations holds contracts with a portfolio of small to medium sized businesses providing them with solutions for all their online marketing needs. They have helped companies in the highly competitive food, auto, and construction markets with the tools and services needed to solidify their online presence and capture new business. Services provided include e-commerce solutions, branding and social media presence, and landing page reformatting and optimization.
